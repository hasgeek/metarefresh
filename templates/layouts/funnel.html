Voting and selection

Participants who have purchased conference tickets will vote on session proposals. The Editorial Panel steering Meta Refresh will also vote on submissions and balance the community voting.

Theme

For 2014 our emphasis is on

  1. front-end workflow process and automation
  2. To push the envelope on the web, designers & front-end engineers need to step out of their comfort zone and exchange skills. MetaRefresh is the place where these 2 ends of the spectrums converge. the idea is not to "make them work together better", the idea is that you need to be both if you want to push the web forward. front-end engineers should think about user experience (attributes like performance which affect user experience) and designers have to start exploiting the medium, front-end engineers need to start thinking about polish, fit & finish

Travel and accomodation for speakers

Attendance is free for selected speakers. HasGeek will offer a grant to cover your travel to and accommodation in Bangalore from anywhere in the world for speakers delivering full sessions (30 minutes or longer). As our budget is limited, we will prefer speakers from locations closer home, but will do our best to cover for anyone exceptional. If you are able to raise support for your trip, we will count that as a speaker travel sponsorship.

Travel grants are reimbursed at the end of the conference. You are expected to make your own travel and stay arrangements. We will assist with hotel recommendations, visa letters and general advice on travel.
